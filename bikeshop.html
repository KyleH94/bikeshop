Welcome to Korrington Bike Shop

Since 1967 we've been serving the residents of Little Rock with all of their cycling needs. We are a a locally-established and independantly owned shop that carries the latest gear and equipment for riders of all levels. Come in for a quick tuneup, or use our
Build-A-Bike system to start riding that day. Enjoy!

Come in and buy a complete bike, or allow our team of professionals to help you build the ride that will suit you the best with our coveted Build-A-Bike system.

Here at Korrington Bike Shop we take safety very seriously. Come on in and take a look at inventory of helmets and safety gear, including products by brands like Bell, Arai, and Shoei.

Need some new spokes, or have a busted handlebar? We carry everything needed to fix your ride up in a jiffy. Thinking of upgrading your gear? We are always up-to-date with the newest tires, frames, and popular cycling accessories.
